Baskup

iMessage Backup application on OS X
Download




iMessage Backup

Baskup creates .txt file backups for each of your conversations and group messages in iMessage




Secure

All your data is protected. Baskup will never collect any information from you




Contact Recognition

Baskup matches phone numbers to contacts in your address book for easy identification of messages




Timestamps

Each message is dated with a timestamp




Free

Baskup is free! But donations help keep the lights on
